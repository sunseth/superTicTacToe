Instructions
Welcome to Super Tic-Tac-Toe! 9 individual tic-tac-toe boards form a larger board. Standard Tic-Tac-Toe rules apply - for the small boards that is.

Introducing Super Tic-Tac-Toe:

  • Win a board, you get that board. That whole board is yours.
  • Win three boards in a row - diagonally, straight up/down or sideways - you win the game.
  • Your opponents move determines where you play. The first move goes anywhere. From there on out, the spot you mark on the small board is the corresponding small board the next player must play in.
  • If you are directed to play in a board that has been taken, any move is now valid!
  • (The board will light up the next move possibilities for you; if no boards are highlighted, you have any available square to choose from)

Strategy and Tips

  • Controlling the center of the board was important in regular Tic-Tac-Toe; it's important in this game as well. Be wary of playing in middle positions. Try to out maneuver your opponent.
  • That being said, don't be afraid of making sacrifices. Sometimes you will have to give up entire boards for the larger picture.
